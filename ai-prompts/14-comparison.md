## When to Use myTech.Today RMM

### Ideal Use Cases
- Small to medium businesses (10-500 endpoints)
- Internal IT departments
- Budget-conscious organizations
- PowerShell-proficient teams
- Windows-primary environments
- Learning/training environments

### When to Consider Commercial RMM
- MSPs with multi-tenant requirements
- Organizations needing mobile apps
- Complex billing/ticketing integration
- Cross-platform (Mac/Linux) primary
- Compliance-heavy industries (pre-built reports)
- 24/7 vendor support required
